Pumpkin Spice Latte

Home

Autumn is the season for pumpkins and candy corn. This recipe will embark you on an adventure to seize your autumn desires. Follow the instructions below to a TEA and you will be able to enjoy a nicely pixelated drink like the one above.

    Ingredients
  • Hot Whole Milk
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Pumpkin Pie Spice
  • Brewed Coffee
  • Whipped Cream
    Steps
  1. Blend + froth hot milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and pumpkin pie spice
  2. Pour mixture into a few mugs about half way full
  3. Add a couple ounces of coffee into each mug
  4. Garnish mugs with whipped cream and extra pumpkin pie spice
