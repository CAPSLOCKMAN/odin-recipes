Pumpkin Pie

Autumn is the season for pumpkins and candy corn. This recipe will embark you on an adventure to seize your autumn desires. Follow the instructions below to a TEA and you will be able to enjoy a nicely pixelated slices pf pie like the one above.

    Ingredients
  • Pie Crust
  • Pumpkin Puree
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Eggs
  • Pumpkin Pie Spice
    Steps
  1. Combine pumpkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, and pumpkin pie spice in large container
  2. Mix until smooth
  3. Pour into pie crust
  4. Toss into 425 degree F oven until filling is set
