Pumpkin Cannoli

Autumn is the season for pumpkins and candy corn. This recipe will embark you on an adventure to seize your autumn desires. Follow the instructions below to a TEA and you will be able to enjoy a nicely pixelated cannolis like the one above.

    Ingredients
  • Confectioners' Sugar
  • Canned Pumpkin
  • Mascarpone Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Instant Vanilla Pudding Mix
  • Pumpkin Pie Spice
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Large Cannoli Shells
  • Sprinkles
    Steps
  1. Mix Cofentioners' sugar, pumpkin, mascarpone cheese, ricotta cheese, pudding mix, and pumpkin pie spice until smooth
  2. Beat cream in a chilled bowl with an electric mixer
  3. Fold into pumpkin mixture
  4. Pipe pumpkin mixture into each cannoli shell
  5. Garnish ends with sprinkles and confectioners' sugar
